A Reduntant Mini Autobiography about Myself

I was born in the Netherlands and raised in the Finger Lakes region of New York. Furthermore, I have lived, studied and/or worked in Ecuador, Italy, South Korea, Switzerland and Poland. After earning my MFA in Sculpture from the Maryland Institute College of Art I had a residency in Zurich, Switzerland in 2007. I currently live in Sunnyside, Queens and work as an ESL (English as a Second Language) teacher in Flushing.
